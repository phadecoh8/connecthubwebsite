About ConnectHub

ConnectHub was founded with a simple mission: to bring people together in meaningful ways. We believe that social media should enhance real connections, not replace them.

Our platform combines the best features of modern social networking with a focus on authentic engagement, privacy, and user experience. Whether you're looking to stay in touch with friends, grow your business, or discover new passions, ConnectHub provides the tools and community to make it happen.

Join us on this journey to create a more connected, empathetic, and inspiring online community.

Getting Started

Creating an Account

To get started with ConnectHub, you'll need to create an account:

  1. Click the "Sign Up" button in the header
  2. Enter your email address and choose a secure password
  3. Verify your email address through the confirmation link
  4. Complete your profile with basic information
  5. Start connecting with friends and exploring content

Basic Navigation

Once logged in, you'll have access to:

  • Home Feed: Your personalized content stream
  • Profile: Your personal page and settings
  • Messages: Direct communication with connections
  • Discover: Explore new content and users
  • Notifications: Stay updated on activity

Developer API

Authentication

ConnectHub uses OAuth 2.0 for API authentication. To get started, you'll need to register your application and obtain API credentials.

POST /api/v1/oauth/token
Content-Type: application/json

{
  "client_id": "your_client_id",
  "client_secret": "your_client_secret",
  "grant_type": "client_credentials"
}

Example API Request

Once authenticated, you can make requests to our API endpoints:

GET /api/v1/users/profile
Authorization: Bearer your_access_token

Response:
{
  "id": "user123",
  "username": "johndoe",
  "email": "john@example.com",
  "created_at": "2024-01-01T00:00:00Z"
}

Rate Limits

API requests are rate-limited to ensure fair usage:

  • Free tier: 1,000 requests per hour
  • Pro tier: 10,000 requests per hour
  • Enterprise: Custom limits based on agreement

Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email address.

How do I change my privacy settings?

Navigate to Settings > Privacy where you can control who can see your posts, profile information, and contact you.

Can I export my data?

Yes, you can request a data export from Settings > Privacy > Download Your Data. The export will be prepared and sent to your email within 24 hours.

How do I report inappropriate content?

Click on the three-dot menu on any post or profile and select "Report". Our moderation team reviews all reports within 24 hours.
